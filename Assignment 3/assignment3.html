Part B: Model Matrix (5 points)

  • Download the program InteractiveCube that will Render a 3D cube in WebGL. 
  • Implement the following: 
    • Use a model matrix uniform (mat4) in the shader to control the rotation and translation of the cube 
    • Hold W, A, S, and D, to move the cube up, left, down, and right. It should move smoothly and continuously while you continue to hold the key. 
    • Click and drag to rotate the cube 
      • Use the mousemove event and event.movementX and event.movementY to rotate the cube 
      • The cube should only rotate while the left mouse button in pressed and the mouse is moving 
      • The X movement of the mouse should rotate the cube about the world Y axis 
      • The Y movement of the mouse should rotate the cube about the world X axis 
      • The direction of the rotation should feel natural and go in the direction of your mouse movement
